Join Us!

We are always happy to hear from curious and creative post-docs, PhD or MSc students who are interested in becoming part of the lab. We welcome experimentalists, computational biologists, or hybrids with a bit of both!
Check the opportunities below and contact us to discuss your ideas and potential funding applications.
Open Positions
We are currently recruiting the first PhD student of the lab. Are you interested in cancer development, single-cell biology, and unraveling the link between driver mutations and cell behaviour? Reach out, we will have an ad coming soon! Informal enquiries via email are welcome, but applications should be submitted via the portal.
Additional Opportunities

Postdoctoral applicants: If you are interested in joining us and we do not have an Open Position listed above, check the postdoc funding opportunities listed here and reach out via email. I am happy to discuss the best strategy to apply for a fellowship to support your time in the lab.

PhD students: If you are interested in joining the group and we do not have an Open Position listed above, check out the Doctoral Training Programs listed here to see if we are offering any project this year.
Moreover, QMUL participates in international PhD programs with specific countries. Check out the list at this link, at the section "International PhD Scholarships" and reach out if you are eligible and interested in applying.

QMUL MSc students: We might be able to host students for projects in the lab. Email us if you are interested, writing a short cover letter that highlights why you would like to join us and your time commitment.
We also offer MSc projects for Bioinformatics or Molecular Cell Biology students: check with your module organiser if we are offering a project this year.
